About Us

Who We Are

Alpha Development Team (ADT) is a SMEDAN-registered community-based organisation headquartered in Maiduguri, Borno State, Nigeria. Founded with a vision of inclusive development, ADT works at the intersection of education, digital innovation, skills empowerment, and environmental sustainability.

We operate within one of Nigeria's most complex humanitarian and development landscapes — the Lake Chad Basin — where years of conflict, displacement, and climate change have created deep vulnerabilities. ADT responds with community-rooted solutions that are practical, dignified, and lasting.

What We Do

Our Programs & Projects

ADT implements a portfolio of interconnected programs designed to address the root causes of vulnerability and build sustainable community resilience.

📚

Education Access Program

Increasing enrolment and retention of children in schools across Borno State. We support learning materials, teacher training, and safe learning environments for displaced and out-of-school children.

Ongoing Children & Youth
💻

Digital Literacy Initiative

Training youth and community members in foundational digital skills — from basic computer use to internet safety, productivity tools, and emerging technologies — for economic participation.

Ongoing Youth & Adults
🛠️

Skills Acquisition & Entrepreneurship

Vocational training in tailoring, ICT, construction, and agribusiness. Paired with entrepreneurship coaching to help participants launch micro-enterprises and generate sustainable income.

Ongoing Youth & Women
🌱

Great Green Wall — Afforestation

In partnership with the Borno State Agency for the Great Green Wall, ADT leads community tree-planting, land restoration, and climate awareness campaigns to combat desertification in the Sahel.

Ongoing Climate Action
🌾

Climate Resilience & Food Security (GEF)

A proposed GEF Small Grants Programme project targeting IDP communities in Borno State with climate-smart agriculture, community gardens, and food security interventions.

Proposed IDPs · Food Security
🤝

Community Mobilisation

Grassroots community dialogue, civic engagement, and social cohesion activities that strengthen local institutions and empower communities to drive their own development.
